about

I’m a Filmmaker specialized in Video editing, motion graphics and post-production in general. I've been working in the audiovisual field for 10 years and I have hundreds of works under my belt. Since then I have worked for media, advertising agencies, music videos among others, both as a freelancer or with studios/companies. I find comfortable working in a team, contributing in a constructive way and compromising with the necessary tasks. I keep constant learning in design tools and resources to improve my skills and to be able to face new different kinds of work.
